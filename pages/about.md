## About the Collection

This collection features videos and photos of various libraries across the Norther suburbs of Chicago. The Northern suburbs of Chicago are notoriously home to some of the most affluent communities in the country and this wealth is explemified in their libraries. This collection is meant to highlight the interesting architecture, vast collections, and beautiful art at the libraries in these communities. 

## About the Author

McKayla Conner grew up in Cedar Rapids, IA where she developed her love for reading, art, and architecture by often visiting the Cedar Rapids Public Library. After moving away from home for the first time, McKayla found solace in visiting the local libraries of her a new community and thus a new tradition was born. McKayla moved to Chicago in 2021 and lived in the surrounding area until 2025. During this time, she spent her freetime visiting and capturing photos and videos of some of the many different libraries in the Northern suburbs of Chicago. Little did she know, she would be using these videos and photos to build a collection highlighting her explorations to encourage others to visit as well as remininse on a previous time in her life.
